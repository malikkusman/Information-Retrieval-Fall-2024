Machine learning is a field of AI.
Deep learning algorithms are a subset of machine learning.
Artificial intelligence in healthcare is growing rapidly.
Natural language processing is a subfield of AI focused on the interaction between computers and human languages.
Reinforcement learning is an area of machine learning that deals with how software agents ought to take actions in an environment.
Computer vision enables machines to interpret and make decisions based on visual data from the world.
AI ethics is an emerging field that examines the moral implications of artificial intelligence.
